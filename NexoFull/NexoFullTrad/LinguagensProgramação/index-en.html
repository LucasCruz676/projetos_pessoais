What are Programming Languages?

Programming languages are the means by which developers instruct computers to perform tasks. With them, it's possible to create everything from simple websites to complex artificial intelligence systems, games, mobile applications, and enterprise software.

Types of Programming Languages

There are several programming languages, each with its specific characteristics and purposes. They are divided into two main groups:

High-Level Language

Closer to human language, like Python, Java, and JavaScript. Easy to learn, ideal for diverse projects.

  • Easier to learn
  • Hardware abstraction
  • Increased productivity
Low-Level Language

Closer to machine language, like Assembly and C. Used in systems that require maximum performance.

  • High performance
  • Direct hardware control
  • Less abstraction
