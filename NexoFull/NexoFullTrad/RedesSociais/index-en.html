Social Media

Social media are online platforms for connection and sharing of information, ideas, photos and videos, promoting interactions between people. They act as digital squares where friends and strangers exchange news and form communities.

The Evolution of Social Media

The First Social Media Platform

SixDegrees (1997) - Considered the first modern social media platform, it allowed users to create profiles, add friends and browse connection lists. Based on the six degrees of separation theory, it was a pioneer but closed in 2001.

The Most Used Social Media in History

Facebook - With over 3 billion monthly active users, Facebook became the social media platform with the largest accumulated user base in history. Founded by Mark Zuckerberg in 2004, it revolutionized how people connect online.

The Most Used Platform Currently

WhatsApp - With over 2.7 billion active users, WhatsApp is currently the most used social media platform globally. In some countries like Brazil and India, over 90% of the population with internet access uses the app daily.

Creators Who Shaped Social Media

Mark Zuckerberg - Facebook (2004)

Kevin Systrom & Mike Krieger - Instagram (2010)

Jack Dorsey - Twitter (2006)

Jan Koum & Brian Acton - WhatsApp (2009)

Zhang Yiming - TikTok (2016)

Chad Hurley, Steve Chen & Jawed Karim - YouTube (2005)

The Impact of Social Media

Social media have completely transformed human communication. Today, more than 4.8 billion people use social media globally, spending an average of 2.5 hours per day on these platforms. They influence everything from personal relationships to politics, economy and culture.
